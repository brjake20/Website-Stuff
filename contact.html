§What we can help with

  • Bookings & reservations — questions about availability, group sizes, or rescheduling.
  • Private events & parties — birthdays, corporate outings, team-building, and full-venue buyouts.
  • Accessibility — accommodations for guests with mobility, sensory, or other needs. We want everyone to have a great time, so please reach out in advance and we'll work with you.
  • Lost & found — items left behind during a visit. Email is fastest.
  • Press & partnerships — please use email and include the subject line "Press" or "Partnership."

§Response times

Email is the fastest way to reach us for non-urgent questions. We typically respond within 1–2 business days. For same-day booking questions or anything urgent, please call.

Already have a booking and need to cancel or reschedule? See our cancellation & refund policy for windows and details, then call or email and we'll take care of it.

§Business information

Raven & Dragon is operated by Raven and Dragon Entertainment LLC, in partnership with Bella Vista Enterprises LLC. Our venue is located on historic Route 66 in Williams, Arizona — the gateway to the Grand Canyon.
